Hello!
Welcome to my website. I am a third year Mechanical Engineering PhD student at the MANE department of Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute (RPI), Troy, NY, supervised by Prof. Matthew Adam Oehlschlaeger. Broadly, I am interested in automated instruemtation and sensing systmes. I develop nearest neighbors, random forest, neural network and support vector machine based frameworks for supervised learning of patterns from measurements to make experiments automated. As part of my PhD research I am also working on gas sensing methods based on THz sensing technology.

Previously, I obtained my Master's degree from at Indiana University Purdue University Indianapolis, supervised by Prof. Mohamed Razi Nalim. I earned my Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering from BUET, Bangladesh.
